ACF's Social Media Analysis

Evidence from Twitter


(@AusConservation)

Last update:12/10/2015

Summery

  • ACF(@AusConservation) has been active on Twitter since December 2008.
  • The account has posted 9,495 tweets (posts) since then and several tweets are posted most days.
  • There are currently 18,575 people following ACF's posts.

Original Posts

The original tweets posted by ACF have been reposted\forwarded (retweeted) by other users very differently. Although ACF's tweets are retweeted 6.6 times on average, some posts have been retweeted up to 195 times.

Timing

Most tweets are tweeted between 9AM to 7PM while the ones posted late night or early morning have been retweeted more on average.

Picture or No Picture

Tweets with a photo attached are retweeted and favored around two times more compared the tweets without a photo
